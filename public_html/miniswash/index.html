The whole unit is supposed to run off of 9v DC though a 7809 regulator, which drops a couple of volts off the input. So then the unit is really "supposed" to run off of 7-something volts, though the op amps are spec'd for 9. I put this in there, sort of as an experiment. It's a 7809 wired for 12v input, with reverse polarity. There's still a knob on the top side to adjust the power to lower levels.
